Himachali Rasoi

Top choice Himachal in Shimla

It's just four heavy wooden tables crammed into a bazaar-box shop-unit but the metalcraft lamps add character and the deliciously flavoured thalis (multicurry tray-meals) are a wonderful introduction to local Himachal vegetarian food.
