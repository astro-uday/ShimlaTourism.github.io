SHIMLA


Strung out along a 12km ridge, with steep forested hillsides falling away in all directions, the Himachal capital is one of India's most popular hill resorts, buzzing with a happy flow of heat-escaping Indian vacationers.
Beautifully located at the perfect height of about 2200mtrs in Himachal Pradesh, Shimla is the biggest and most popular city in the whole Hmachal. This city was beautifully designed by different foreigner rulers, Shimla has " THE RIDGE, JAKHU TEMPLE, KUFRI, CHAIL, THE CHURCH, SUMMER HILL, and a number of countless "BREATHTAKING & HEARTWARMING" places that you'll endup seeing once you visit this beautiful distt. of Himachal Pradesh !

The tour for shimla is very worth for the price you give for the tour you want..
