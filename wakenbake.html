Wake and Bake

Top choice Multicuisine in Shimla

On two upper floors via an almost invisibly narrow stairway, this loveably cosy coffee shop serves espressos made from organic South Indian beans, but also cakes, sweet and savoury pancakes, hummus, falafel, toasties, veg stir-fry, pasta and kathi (a Kolkata street-food dish) rolls. Various breakfast options, too. Wi-fi is free.
